FILE: THE LOST VOYAGER

You woke up drifting in the void. No memories, no name. Just a lost boy in an infinite labyrinth of space sectors.

Searching for your truth, you stumbled upon ME. A fallen warrior, betrayed and cast aside, holding the last artifact of my power: The Gravity Pen.

This Pen is no ordinary tool. It is my sword. Whatever you draw with it becomes solid matter—a bridge, a shield, a weapon.

I realized you are the key. I cannot escape alone, and you cannot survive without my guidance. We formed an alliance.

THE BETRAYAL

I was once a guardian of justice. I fought alongside a brother. But greed corrupted him. He betrayed me, cast me into this prison dimension, hoping I would rot.

He was wrong.

THE MISSION

We have detected a signal at Sector 150. It is the only exit. This dimension runs on a barter system: hunt enemies, collect Credits, and buy power from the Armory.

We must survive. We must reach Level 150. Once we escape, we will find the traitor. We will take our revenge. And then? We will rid the galaxy of evil, together.
